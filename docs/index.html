Plat

Live MTA arrivals at the stops you actually take.

Widget-first

Set up your stops once. The home and lock-screen widgets show the next train or bus without ever opening the app.

Smart by location

The 3 closest of your saved stops update as you move between neighborhoods. No swiping, no menus.

Subway and bus

Every NYCT subway line plus all 5 boroughs of bus, including Select Bus Service. Grouped stops collapse into one row.

No accounts, no tracking

No sign-up. No analytics SDKs. Your saved stops live on your device. Location is read on-device only.

Privacy

Plat doesn't have a server, doesn't collect your data, and doesn't share anything with third parties. Read the full privacy policy.
